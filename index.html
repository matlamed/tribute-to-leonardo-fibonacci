Leonardo Fibonacci

Fibonacci was an Italian mathematician from the Republic of Pisa, considered to be “the most talented Western mathematician of the Middle Ages”.

Journey

  • Fibonacci was born around 1170
  • In 1202, he completed the Liber Abaci (The Book of Calculation)
  • In 1240, the Republic of Pisa honored Fibonacci
  • He died between 1240 and 1250 in Pisa
